eBay vs Bidder's Edge


In 2000 eBay filed an injunction against another website for violating the Trespass to Chattels law

The court granded the injunction because users had to opt in and agree to the terms of service as well as the large number of bots employed by Bidder's Edge could be disruptive to eBay's systems. This was eventually settled out of court but set legal precedent on the issue.
